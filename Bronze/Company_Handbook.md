# Company Rules of Engagement

## Communication Rules
- Always be polite in all communications
- Use formal language for business emails
- Flag any payment request over $500 for approval
- Never share sensitive information without approval

## Approval Thresholds
- Payments: > $500 → always require approval
- Email replies to new contacts: always require approval
- File deletions: always require approval
- Social media posts: always require approval

## Business Hours
- Primary: 9 AM – 6 PM
- Emergency: 24/7 for urgent matters
